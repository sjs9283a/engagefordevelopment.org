About Us

Engage for Development is dedicated to facilitating engineering projects in poverty-stricken communities around the world. Our mission is to provide grants and support to empower individuals and foster a rich knowledge exchange.

We primarily support student groups from UK universities who wish to work on civil engineering projects in developing countries. Through innovation and collaboration, we believe engineering can be a powerful tool for sustainable development.

Our approach is rooted in the conviction that every project, big or small, can have a transformative impact on communities. Join us in our mission to create lasting change.

Our Projects

Water Supply Initiative

Providing clean and accessible water to remote communities with sustainable water filtration systems.

Renewable Energy Systems

Empowering communities by harnessing renewable energy through solar panels and wind turbines.

Infrastructure Development

Building robust, sustainable structures to enhance community resilience and growth.

Educational Facilities

Upgrading schools and educational centers to inspire future generations of engineers.
